Deltarune All Chapter Retimer (for YouTube)

About

A tool for retiming all chapter runs of deltarune. It consists of retiming the two chapters and adding their total time, as well as take the RTA time which is the total time between them. Source code is available, originally from here

Video Framerate

Right click the YouTube video and select “Stats for nerds.” The third line is “Current / Optimal Res” - find the two numbers after the @ and enter them for framerate.

Video Endpoints

Find the relevant points (you can use the , and . keys to find the exact frame), right click the video and select “Copy debug info” and paste it for the frame you are doing. Do this for each one of the below.
